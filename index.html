How it works: your script is encrypted byte-by-byte with a random key generated fresh on every run, then base64-packed into a small Luau loader stub with randomized variable names. The stub decrypts and runs the payload with loadstring at runtime — the original source never appears as plaintext anywhere in the output. Encryption happens entirely in your browser; nothing is uploaded anywhere.
